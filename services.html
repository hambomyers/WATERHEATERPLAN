Hard & Acidic Water Solutions

Virginia's Shenandoah Valley is known for hard and acidic water conditions that accelerate water heater deterioration. Our Advanced plans include specialized care for these challenging water conditions.

Advanced Water Care Includes:

  • Bi-Annual Service Visits: Two complete inspections per year to combat aggressive water conditions.
  • Water Quality Testing: Professional analysis of pH, hardness, and mineral content.
  • Circulation Pump Inspection: Check recirculation systems for scale buildup and efficiency.
  • Minor Repairs Covered: Up to $100/year in minor repairs included (valves, connections, minor leaks).
  • Treatment Consultation: Expert recommendations for water softeners or acid neutralizers.

Future Water Treatment Services

We're expanding to offer complete water treatment solutions including acid neutralizer installation ($500-$1,000) and whole-home water softening systems. Ask about pre-booking discounts for these upcoming services.
